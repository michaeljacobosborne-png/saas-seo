The workflow

From blank page to ranked article

Most teams use four or five tools to do this. Byline runs it in one place, each step building on the last.

1

Find the right keyword

Tell the agent what you want to rank for. It asks a few questions, then pulls volume, difficulty, intent, and competitor clusters from DataForSEO. You get a seed list you can actually use.

2

Build the brief

Byline reads the top-ranking pages and builds a research brief: H2 structure, secondary keywords, competitor gaps, AEO questions. You review it and set the word count before anything generates.

3

Write the article

GPT-4o writes the draft in your editor. If it falls short of the target, a second pass identifies expansion angles and adds real research. It doesn't pad. It builds.

4

Score it, fix it, ship it

You get four live scores: SEO, readability, AEO, GEO. The agent reads the article, quotes the problem, and rewrites on request. One conversation from draft to ready.
